
# Chatake Innoworks eLearn

**Chatake Innoworks eLearn** is an independent, academic-grade e-learning platform
designed for structured teaching and learning in engineering, data science,
artificial intelligence, and emerging technologies.

This platform is developed and maintained under **Chatake Innoworks** and is
not affiliated with any university, board, or regulatory body.

---

## Vision

To create a clean, structured, and rigorous digital learning environment that
mirrors the seriousness of a university Learning Management System (LMS), while
remaining fully open, flexible, and self-owned.

---

## What eLearn Provides

- Subject-wise structured learning modules
- Unit-wise theory notes and presentations
- Practicals and hands-on exercises
- Microprojects aligned with academic and industry needs
- Assessments such as weekly tests, class tests, and prelims
- Curated learning resources including talks and podcasts

---

## Platform Philosophy

- Academic clarity over content overload
- Structure before scale
- Long-term reusability and independence
- Clear separation between content creation and content delivery

---

## Repository Scope

This repository hosts:

- Platform structure and navigation
- Public-facing academic content
- Subject-level organisation

Raw teaching material, drafts, and working files are maintained separately and
are selectively integrated into this platform.
