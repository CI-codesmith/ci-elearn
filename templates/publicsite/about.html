About Chatake Innoworks

Engineering Ideas. Empowering Innovation.

Who We Are

Chatake Innoworks Pvt. Ltd. is an innovative AI–IT research and development startup dedicated to building intelligent systems through the seamless integration of Artificial Intelligence, robotics, and automation technologies. We are committed to transforming theoretical knowledge into practical, deployment-ready solutions that address real-world challenges across industries.

Our family-rooted organization bridges the critical gap between classroom education and corporate innovation, fostering an ecosystem where academic research meets industrial application. We believe in nurturing talent and creating pathways for continuous learning and technological advancement.

Core Philosophy

"AI begins at home."

This guiding principle reflects our commitment to democratizing artificial intelligence and making it accessible, understandable, and applicable from the grassroots level. We bridge the gap between classroom education and corporate innovation by creating meaningful connections between theoretical frameworks and practical implementations.

Our Approach

We emphasize end-to-end system integration encompassing sensor data acquisition, embedded systems development, and deployment-ready software platforms that deliver measurable results. Our methodology integrates:

Data Acquisition

Sensor networks and IoT devices for real-time data collection

Edge Computing

Embedded systems and intelligent device management

AI Intelligence

Machine learning models and advanced algorithms

Platform Deployment

Scalable cloud infrastructure and application hosting

Strategic Divisions

Our organization operates through four specialized divisions, each contributing unique expertise to our comprehensive AI–IT ecosystem:

MindforgeAI

The R&D engine driving innovation forward. Advanced AI research, Deep Learning applications, and Robotics & Automation integration.

CodeSmith Systems

EduSphere
