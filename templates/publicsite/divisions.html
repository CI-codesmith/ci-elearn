Our Divisions

Integrated solutions across education, coaching, internships, and projects

CI-Elearn

Comprehensive Learning Management System for colleges and educational institutions. CI-Elearn provides all tools needed to manage courses, deliver content, assess students, and track academic progress.

Key Features:

  • Course management and batch organization
  • Digital content delivery and assignments
  • Assessment and grading tools
  • Student progress tracking and analytics
  • Enrollment management
  • Institutional reporting and records

CI-EduSphere

Coaching and tutoring platform designed for academic preparation centers, skill development programs, and institutional training. Manage batches, schedules, assessments, and student performance with a unified interface.

Key Features:

  • Batch and class management
  • Schedule and timetable management
  • Performance assessment and analytics
  • Student tracking and progress reports
  • Resource and material management
  • Parent and stakeholder communication

CI-Internship

End-to-end internship program management platform. Connect students with professional opportunities, track progress, manage evaluations, and build professional networks through structured internship programs.

Key Features:

  • Internship opportunity listing and application
  • Student profile and skill matching
  • Progress tracking and task management
  • Mentor and supervisor interface
  • Performance evaluation tools
  • Certificate and credential management

CI-Projects

Project management and team collaboration platform for managing complex initiatives, organizing teams, tracking deliverables, and ensuring accountability across multiple concurrent projects.

Key Features:

  • Project creation and organization
  • Team member management
  • Milestone and deliverable tracking
  • Task assignment and workflow management
  • Progress visibility and reporting
  • Collaboration tools and communication

CI-Accounts

Comprehensive financial and administrative management module. Handle fee management, payments, accounting, invoicing, and institutional administrative tasks with institutional-grade control and audit trails.

Key Features:

  • Fee and payment management
  • Receipt and invoice generation
  • Accounting and ledger management
  • Financial reporting and analytics
  • Payment gateway integration
  • Audit trails and compliance
