# Practical Evaluation

Practicals are evaluated primarily on participation and understanding,
rather than optimisation or performance.

---

## Evaluation Criteria

Practicals are assessed based on:

- Completion of required steps
- Correct execution of procedures
- Ability to interpret results
- Quality of reflections and observations

---

## Weightage

- Low to moderate weightage
- Practicals support learning rather than ranking
- Completion is prioritised over perfection

---

## Feedback

- Feedback focuses on correcting misconceptions
- Emphasis on conceptual clarity
- Used to prepare learners for microprojects
