# Weekly Tests

Weekly tests are **formative assessments** designed to reinforce
understanding of recently covered units.

---

## Purpose

- Encourage regular study habits
- Test conceptual understanding
- Identify misconceptions early

---

## Structure

- Short duration (15â€“30 minutes)
- Conceptual and reasoning-based questions
- No heavy computation
- Focus on definitions, intuition, and interpretation

---

## Coverage

- Typically mapped to one unit
- May include short reflective questions
- Can include MCQs, short answers, or reasoning prompts

---

## Evaluation

- Low weightage
- Feedback-focused
- Used to guide teaching pace and depth
