# Microproject Guidelines

This document outlines the general guidelines for Machine Learning
microprojects under Chatake Innoworks eLearn.

---

## Scope

- Microprojects should address a single core concept
- Datasets, if used, should be small and interpretable
- External libraries may be used, but understanding must be demonstrated

---

## Deliverables

Typical microproject deliverables include:

- A brief problem description
- Explanation of approach and assumptions
- Implementation (code or pseudocode)
- Interpretation of results
- Reflection on limitations

---

## Collaboration

- Microprojects are primarily individual tasks
- Discussion is encouraged, but submissions must be original
- Proper attribution is required for external references

---

## Ethics and Responsibility

- Datasets should be used responsibly
- Biases and limitations should be acknowledged
- Results should not be overstated
