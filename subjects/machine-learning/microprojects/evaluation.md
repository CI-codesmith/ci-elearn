# Microproject Evaluation

Microprojects are evaluated using qualitative and quantitative criteria
focused on understanding and reasoning.

---

## Evaluation Criteria

Microprojects are assessed based on:

- Clarity of problem formulation
- Correctness of approach
- Quality of interpretation
- Awareness of assumptions and limitations
- Overall coherence and presentation

---

## Weightage

- Microprojects carry moderate weightage
- Emphasis is placed on learning outcomes rather than perfection
- Partial credit is awarded for sound reasoning

---

## Feedback

- Feedback focuses on conceptual understanding
- Strengths and improvement areas are highlighted
- Feedback is intended to guide future learning
